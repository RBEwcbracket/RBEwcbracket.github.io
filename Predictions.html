Round of 32 Predictions

Registration Details:
  • Username: Choose a name that will appear on the leaderboard.
  • PIN: Select a 4-digit PIN. This acts as a simple "key" to ensure your entry isn't accidentally modified by others. Please note this is not a secure password, so don't use a PIN you use for your bank or sensitive accounts.
How to enter your predictions:
  • Final Score: Enter the score at the end of 90 minutes + extra time. Penalty shootouts do not count toward this score.
  • Tie Scenario: If you predicted a tie, i.e., game goes to penalties, you must select the team you believe will win the resulting penalty shootout.
How points are awarded for round of 32 predictions:
  • Correct Winner: 7 Points
  • Exact Score Bonus: +5 Points (only if winner is correct)
  • Goal Differential Bonus: +2 Points (if winner is correct, but score is not exact)
Submission:
  • After entering your predictions, click the submit button
  • If you submit multiple times, only the most recent submission will be used for your predictions
  • Latest you can submit is Sunday 2:30pm EDT (30 minutes before the start of the first game of round of 32)
